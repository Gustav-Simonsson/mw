Real Bitcoin Bets on the Football World Cup 2014 in Brazil

This site allows you to place real, decentralized, oracle-controlled, trust-free bets using your Bitcoins the way they were supposed to.

Multi-signature contracts are created for your benefit in the browser, using Open Source JavaScript and one-time key pairs to lock waged amounts into the blockchain.

After the match, winner keys are issued that allow the winner to cash in on them Multisig transactions that constitute the bets and transfer the price into their wallet.

This site is merely offering help with the scripts, at no point is the money passing through our hands. Even if this site did not exist anymore after the World Cup, the bets were still safely resolvable through the Bitcoin blockchain.
